Our Story

Mori More was born from a passion for authentic Japanese matcha culture. We believe in celebrating the art of matcha—from tea ceremonies to contemporary culinary expressions. Our journey began in Karlsruhe, where we opened our doors to share this exquisite tradition.

Every product we create honors the meticulous craftsmanship of Japanese artisans, combined with innovative culinary techniques. We're committed to bringing the serene beauty and bold flavors of matcha to our community.

Matcha

Excellence

Our matcha is sourced exclusively from premium-grade suppliers in Kyoto, Japan. We honor traditional preparation methods while embracing contemporary innovation. Every cake, pastry, and beverage tells the story of matcha mastery—a story we're delighted to share with you.

About Mori More

Mori More celebrates the art and tradition of matcha culture. Newly opened in Karlsruhe, we bring authentic Japanese tea and artisanal matcha cakes to our community. Every creation reflects our passion for quality and excellence.
